Company overview

ACE WATER, a rapidly expanding water treatment company, specializes in providing consultancy and EPC turnkey services for water treatment, reclamation and desalination plants in Singapore and the Asia Pacific region. At ACE WATER, we develop innovative yet robust and cost competitive solutions, based on our extensive field practical experiences and in-depth understanding of both conventional and the state of the art technologies, for all water and wastewater challenges both for municipal and industries.

For more information, please visit: www.acewater.com.sg

We are looking for energetic and dynamic candidates to join our team.
